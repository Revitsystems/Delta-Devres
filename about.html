ABOUT DEVRES

Building Solutions Through
Knowledge and Collaboration

DELTA DEVRES International was founded on a simple but powerful belief: better decisions are made when they are grounded in reliable evidence. We work to ensure that decision-makers and stakeholders have access to the right data, understand its meaning, and can confidently use it to shape policies, programs, and systems that deliver real results.

Driven by a commitment to equity, stability, and sustainability, we support institutions at local and global levels to design, implement, and manage development initiatives that create lasting value. Our team combines technical expertise, collaborative innovation, and practical experience to generate solutions that strengthen governance, improve service delivery, and expand opportunities for people and communities.

Our Mission

Our mission is to facilitate, influence and support effective planning and system strengthening initiatives that will transform insight into action aimed at improving service delivery, outcomes and impact on society.

Our Vision

To Promote a Pathway to excellence that will, regulate & enhance standard research and result driven practice that will contribute to the growth and development of organizations and institutions by supporting them to discover untapped potential and making a difference through Evidence-based decision making.

OUR CORE VALUES

Why Organizations Choose Delta Devres

Collaboration

We work across disciplines, sectors, and partners to deliver integrated solutions. By sharing knowledge, ideas, and expertise within our teams and with clients, we ensure that every engagement benefits from diverse perspectives and collective learning.

Efficiency

Innovation

OUR APPROACH

Evidence-Driven Impact

Our approach is grounded in the belief that meaningful and lasting change is achieved when decision-makers have access to credible evidence and the capacity to use it effectively. We apply an integrated, collaborative, and results-driven approach that supports organizations to plan better, strengthen systems, and deliver sustainable outcomes.

01

Evidence at the Center

We work to ensure that stakeholders and decision-makers have access to relevant, high-quality data. Through research, assessments, surveys, and evaluations, we generate evidence that informs planning, policy, and program implementation across development and humanitarian contexts.

02

Integrated and Inclusive Thinking

Structured as a matrix organization, we draw on diverse expertise across sectors, disciplines, and contexts. This allows us to continuously share knowledge, apply good practices, and deliver holistic solutions that reflect the social, economic, and institutional realities of each project.

03

Collaboration with Stakeholders

We actively collaborate with clients, partners, and peers—including governments, NGOs, communities, and institutions—to co-create solutions. Our team-based approach ensures shared ownership, alignment with local priorities, and stronger outcomes.

04

System Strengthening and Capacity Building

Beyond delivering projects, we focus on strengthening systems and building institutional capacity. Through mentoring, training, and advisory support, we help organizations improve processes, enhance performance, and sustain results over time.

05

Adaptive Implementation and Learning

We apply innovative thinking, learn from both successes and challenges, and adapt our approach as projects evolve. By embedding learning and accountability into our work, we continuously improve effectiveness and relevance.

06

Results, Accountability, and Sustainability

We are committed to efficiency, responsibility, and value. Every engagement is designed to maximize impact, use resources responsibly, and contribute to long-term, sustainable development outcomes—supporting safe, inclusive, well-governed, and climate-resilient societies.
